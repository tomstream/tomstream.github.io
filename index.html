I am a master student of Zhejiang University, majoring in Computer Science and Technology. Currently I am a research assistant co-advised by Yang Yang and Fei Wu. I also fortunately have Chenhao Tan from University of Colorado Boulder as my external advisor.

My research focuses on urban computing with heterogeneous data sources:

  • Migrant integration in urbanization
  • Early departure of new migrants
  • Imputation for residents' electricity data
